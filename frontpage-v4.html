What is Innovation for Defence?

I4D is the dedicated internal program accelerating bottom-up, short-cycle innovation within Belgian Defence.

Its core function is to ensure that the vital ideas and practical solutions generated by Belgian Defence‚Äôs operational teams in the field are quickly captured, systematically tested, and integrated into our daily operations.

We exist to provide the agility required to stay ahead in a rapidly changing technological and strategic environment.

Why We Need Inno4Def: Adapting to Change

The operational environment is changing faster than ever. Technology moves quickly, merging civilian and military domains. To maintain our strength and ensure mission success, Belgian Defence must be agile and adaptive.

I4D is not a top-down mandate; it's a dedicated pathway for empowering you. It ensures your critical insights and practical solutions are systematically turned into Defence-wide action.

  • Integrate new solutions: Quickly trial and deploy technologies.
  • Empower our people: Give every member a voice to improve operations.
  • Stay Ahead: Remain at the forefront of operational readiness.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a 'bottom-up' idea?

A bottom-up idea is one that originates directly from personnel on the ground‚Äîthose with daily operational experience‚Äîrather than being mandated from senior leadership (top-down).

Who is on the Innovation Board?

The Innovation Board is a group of dedicated Innovation Officers representing every component and staff level of Belgian Defence. They review, prioritize, and support the implementation of ideas.

How quickly are ideas implemented?

I4D focuses on 'short-cycle' innovation. While implementation speed varies, the process is designed to rapidly test and integrate solutions, often moving from concept to prototype faster than traditional procurement methods.

Can external partners submit ideas?

Yes, external partners are encouraged to submit solution ideas (The Idea Submitter profile) or engage with validated challenges (The Problem Solver profile).
