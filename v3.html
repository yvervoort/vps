Tips & Guidance: How to Formulate a Problem Statement

Define the Problem (Questions 1-5):

  • Focus on the **symptom** and the **underlying root cause**.
  • Clearly describe the **consequences** (wasted effort, delays, data loss).

Find the Opportunity (Question 6):

  • Try to rephrase your problem into a positive, action-oriented **'How Might We'** question.

Next Steps:

  • After submitting, we encourage you to check the **Active Challenges** page.
